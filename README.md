# Portfolio Asset Guide

## Drop your files here:

### profile/
- profile.jpg — your main photo

### university/
- logos/ — university logo
- projects/ — screenshots of academic projects
- events/ — photos from hackathons, events, fests

### work/
- company-logos/ — logos of companies you worked at
- projects/ — screenshots of work projects

### personal/
- projects/ — screenshots of AI/ML personal projects
- youtube-thumbnails/ — thumbnails or channel banner

### certificates/
- university/ — academic certs
- work/ — internship/job certs
- personal/ — course certs (Coursera, Udemy, etc.)

### docs/
- resume.pdf — your latest resume
